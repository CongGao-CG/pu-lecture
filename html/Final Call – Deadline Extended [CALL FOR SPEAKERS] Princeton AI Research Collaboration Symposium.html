The Princeton Laboratory for Artificial Intelligence has extended the deadline for submitting proposals for the upcoming AI Lab Research Collaboration Symposium. Proposals will be accepted until 9 a.m. Wednesday, Sept. 17.


AI continues to unlock new possibilities across a broad spectrum of research domains. This symposium is designed to spotlight areas where AI can drive transformative impact and to foster interdisciplinary collaboration among faculty. 


We invite faculty to submit proposals for two types of short talks:

  • A problem that you would love to solve but haven’t yet figured out how to address and which could benefit from an interdisciplinary collaborator, or

  • A creative solution that is seeking datasets or applications that go beyond your own research community


All talks will be 5-10 minutes long (depending on the final schedule) and should end with a slide outlining concrete opportunities for collaboration.
