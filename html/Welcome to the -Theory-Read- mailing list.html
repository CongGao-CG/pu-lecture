Welcome to the "Theory-Read" mailing list! To post to this list, send your message to: theory-read@lists.cs.princeton.edu You can unsubscribe or make adjustments to your options via email by sending a message to: theory-read-request@lists.cs.princeton.edu with the word 'help' in the subject or body (don't include the quotes), and you will get back a message with instructions. You will need your password to change your options, but for security purposes, this password is not included here. If you have forgotten your password you will need to reset it via the web UI.
