October 1: Dean's Leadership Series: Chiquita Brooks-LaSure '96, Former Administrator of the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services

Join us for a conversation with Chiquita Brooks-LaSure, former Administrator of the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS), on the future of U.S. health care policy. The first Black woman to lead CMS, she oversaw programs serving more than 150 million Americans and brings extensive experience in federal health policy. Brooks-LaSure will discuss the future of Medicare and Medicaid under the current administration, explore policy pathways to strengthen these programs, and share insights on advancing equity and access in U.S. health care.
