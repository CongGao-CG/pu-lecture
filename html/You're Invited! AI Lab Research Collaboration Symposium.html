You’re Invited!

AI Lab Research Collaboration Symposium

12:30-4 p.m. on Friday, Sept. 26 in Simpson B60

Lunch will be provided

Add to Calendar


The Princeton Laboratory for Artificial Intelligence invites faculty members, researchers, and graduate students to the AI Lab Research Collaboration Symposium, a gathering dedicated to exploring new research ideas and finding partners to pursue them.  


Please join us for an afternoon of engaging talks from more than 20 of your colleagues. The symposium will begin with a series of short presentations from researchers seeking interdisciplinary collaborators, followed by longer research talks from faculty members. The event will conclude with updates from AI Lab seed grant recipients on their research projects.


For a full list of speakers and talk titles, please visit our website.
