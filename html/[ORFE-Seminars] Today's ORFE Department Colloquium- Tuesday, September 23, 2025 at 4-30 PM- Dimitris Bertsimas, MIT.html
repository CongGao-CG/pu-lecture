Today’s ORFE Department Colloquium

Date & Time: Tuesday, September 23, 2025 at 4:30 PM

Location: 104 - Computer Science

Speaker: Dimitris Bertsimas, MIT

Title: A vision of Customized and Adaptive Online Education using Gen-AI

Abstract: I present a vision for online education that adapts the context, the duration, the language and the depth of a lecture to the learner. This is a vision of personalized education that, in my opinion, will revolutionize education and will change universities in the years to come.  I outline the current efforts in these directions within Open Learning at MIT.
